The word frequencies table

The word frequencies of a play or all plays of an author are calculated here. For the latter, the checkbox "Use all plays" must be selected in the panel on the left. The word frequencies of a play/all plays of an author are represented below in the form of a table and a word cloud. Options such as stop word consideration, punctuation removal and lowercase conversion of words are in the lower left panel. There are further options for the wordclouds: TODO

Note: For authors with multiple plays, it may take a short time to calculate all word frequencies for all plays by that author.

Word frequency table

The table is sorted by the most frequent words. You can sort the table by its feature names (= unique words), the most/less frequent words and the document frequency. The values in the column "frequency" describe the absolute frequencies of the word. The values in the column "docfreq" describe how often the words occur in a document (= document frequency DF). A word occurs at least in one document and at most in all documents.
